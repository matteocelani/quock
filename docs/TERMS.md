## 4. What Quock is (and is not)

Quock is a **client** — a piece of software that sends Your messages to Ollama Cloud on Your behalf and renders the streaming responses.

- **Quock does NOT host, train, or generate AI responses.** Every response You see was generated by an Ollama-hosted model in response to a request Quock transmitted on Your behalf.
- **Quock does NOT moderate or filter AI Output.** Filtering, safety, and policy enforcement are Ollama's responsibility on the inference side.
- **Quock does NOT have a server-side component.** There is no Quock account. There is no Quock-side database holding Your conversations. Your messages and history live on Your device; the only thing that leaves Your device is the message currently in flight to Ollama.
- **Quock does NOT provide customer support for Ollama-side issues.** Billing, account access, model availability, response quality, content policy enforcement — all of these are Ollama's responsibility. Contact them at [`hello@ollama.com`](mailto:hello@ollama.com).
